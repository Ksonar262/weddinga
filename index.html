Events

1st December

Engagement 4:30PM - 6PM

A beautiful prelude to our journey, the Engagement Ceremony marks the formal announcement of our intent to marry. Surrounded by our loved ones, we will exchange rings, symbolizing our commitment and the beginning of our shared future.

Sangeet & Haldi 7PM onwards

Get ready to kick off the festivities with an evening of pure joy and spirited celebration! Our Sangeet is where the magic truly begins, filled with dazzling music, energetic dance, and boundless merriment. Join us as our families and friends come together to share their happiness through unforgettable performances and joyful revelry. Following this, we invite you to partake in the heartwarming Haldi Ceremony, where a paste of turmeric, oil, and other auspicious ingredients is applied to the bride and groom. This tradition is believed to cleanse, bless, and beautify us, invoking prosperity and good health for our married life, setting the perfect, festive tone for the beautiful days ahead.

2nd December

Wedding Vidhi 8AM-10AM

The absolute heart of our celebration awaits you at the Wedding Vidhi! Here, amidst sacred rituals and ancient hymns, we will solemnize our marriage and embark on our lifelong journey together. It is a moment of profound significance as we take our vows and receive the blessings that will guide our path.

Mangalashtak 12PM onwards

Prepare for a truly moving moment during the Mangalashtak. As eight auspicious verses are recited, showering blessings upon us, we will stand separated by a ceremonial cloth, which is then gracefully removed to signify our beautiful union. It will be a moment of profound emotion and divine grace, marking the cherished beginning of our new life as husband and wife.
